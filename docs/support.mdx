This page outlines Spaire support channels and support expectations.

## Support channels

| Channel | Best for |
| :------ | :------- |
| [Email](mailto:support@spairehq.com) | Account issues, billing questions, migration support, production incidents |
| [Discord](https://dub.sh/spaire-discord) | Community discussion and implementation guidance |

## Email support

For direct support, contact [support@spairehq.com](mailto:support@spairehq.com).

When contacting support, include:

- Your organization identifier
- A clear description of the issue
- Relevant request IDs, timestamps, and error payloads
- Steps to reproduce

## Discord

[Join our Discord](https://dub.sh/spaire-discord) for community discussions and implementation Q&A.

## Etiquette

Communication should remain respectful and professional in all channels. Spaire
may limit or terminate support interactions that are abusive, threatening,
or contain harassing language.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this support policy, including support channels and response
expectations, at any time. Updates are effective when published.
