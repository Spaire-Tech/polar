This page outlines Spaire support channels and support expectations.

## Email support

For direct support, contact [support@spairehq.com](mailto:support@spairehq.com).

When contacting support, include:

- Your organization identifier
- A clear description of the issue
- Relevant request IDs, timestamps, and error payloads
- Steps to reproduce

## Etiquette

Communication should remain respectful and professional in all channels. Spaire may limit or terminate support interactions that are abusive, threatening, or contain harassing language.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this support policy, including support channels and response expectations, at any time. Updates are effective when published.
