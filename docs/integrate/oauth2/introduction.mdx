### OpenID Connect (OAuth2)

Only use our **OpenID Connect** in case you want to act on the behalf of other users via our API, e.g building an app/service for Spaire customers. Otherwise, always use an **Organization Access Token (OAT)** to integrate Spaire for your own service.

Spaire implements the [OpenID Connect specification](https://openid.net/developers/how-connect-works/) to enable third-party authentication. It's a layer on top of the OAuth2 framework aiming at making integration more standard and predictable.

In particular, it comes with a **discovery endpoint** allowing compatible clients to automatically work with the OpenID Connect server. Here is Spaire's one:

[OpenID Configuration](https://api.spairehq.com/.well-known/openid-configuration)
