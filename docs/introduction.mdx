Spaire is the financial infrastructure for SaaS founders and builders who want to sell globally without the friction of international compliance. Whether you're shipping an AI product, a B2B tool, or a dev-focused utility — Spaire handles the parts that aren't your core business.

When you sell through Spaire, we become the legal seller on every transaction. We collect and remit taxes across 36 currencies and 190+ countries, absorb chargeback liability, and handle the compliance obligations that come with selling software globally — so you never have to register for VAT in dozens of countries or build a tax engine yourself.

On top of that, Spaire is your complete billing stack: subscriptions, usage-based pricing, seat-based plans, checkout, customer portal, automated entitlements, and payouts — all in one platform.

## How it fits together

Spaire sits between you and your customers. You define your products and pricing, point your checkout at Spaire, and we handle everything downstream: payment collection, tax calculation and remittance, invoicing, entitlement delivery, and payout operations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create products and prices">
    Define what you're selling — a fixed monthly subscription, a usage-based plan, a one-time purchase, or all three. No code required to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add checkout to your site">
    Share a checkout link, embed a checkout widget, or generate sessions programmatically from your server. Customers pay; Spaire handles the rest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automate post-purchase access">
    Attach benefits to your products. The moment someone subscribes or buys, Spaire automatically provisions their entitlements — no custom fulfillment logic needed for common cases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync billing state to your app">
    Use webhooks or the Customer State API to keep your application in sync with what each customer has purchased and what they're currently allowed to access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw your earnings">
    Connect your bank account once. Your balance accumulates as sales come in, and you can withdraw whenever you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
