## What is Spaire?

Spaire is a Merchant of Record built for SaaS.

We become the legal seller to your customers. That means we manage global tax collection and remittance, handle regulatory obligations, and operate the billing layer behind your product.

You keep building. We run the financial and compliance engine underneath.

## Why SaaS Teams Choose Spaire

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building-columns" title="Global tax and compliance are operationally heavy">
    Cross-border SaaS revenue creates immediate VAT, GST, and sales tax complexity. Spaire centralizes tax calculation, collection, and remittance so your team can stay focused on product and growth.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="gears" title="Billing systems are often fragmented">
    Most teams stitch together payment gateways, tax engines, invoicing, entitlement logic, and support tooling. Spaire provides a unified platform for subscriptions, metered billing, checkout, discounts, and lifecycle management.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="money-bill-transfer" title="Revenue-to-payout latency hurts velocity">
    Traditional flows can delay access to earned funds. Spaire includes payout capabilities so revenue operations and business spending can move faster.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="box-open-full" title="Manual entitlement workflows don't scale">
    License provisioning and entitlement access are automated with built-in benefits and APIs, keeping payment state and user access synchronized.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your Spaire organization">
    Create an account and set up your organization in the Spaire dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model your pricing and products">
    Configure products, prices, subscriptions, and usage meters based on your SaaS packaging strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect checkout and entitlements">
    Launch with hosted checkout links, embedded checkout, or API-driven sessions, then attach benefits for automatic post-purchase access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate API, webhooks, and portal">
    Use Organization Access Tokens, SDKs, and webhooks to sync billing events with your application and internal systems.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live globally with MoR coverage">
    Move to production and sell globally on Spaire's Merchant of Record infrastructure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
