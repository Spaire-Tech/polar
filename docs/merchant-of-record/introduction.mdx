Spaire is a Merchant of Record (MoR) purpose-built for SaaS.

Under this model, your company sells software to Spaire, and Spaire sells to the
end customer. Spaire is the legal seller on the transaction.

## What this means in practice

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tax collection and remittance" icon="receipt">
    Spaire calculates, collects, and remits applicable VAT, GST, and sales tax
    on covered transactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Regulatory responsibility" icon="shield-check">
    As seller-of-record, Spaire handles obligations tied to the sale and
    interfaces with tax and regulatory processes for those transactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Global expansion support" icon="earth-europe">
    SaaS teams can launch in more markets without standing up local tax
    operations in every jurisdiction first.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Operational focus" icon="rocket-launch">
    Founders and product teams stay focused on shipping product, while Spaire
    manages core revenue compliance workflows.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## MoR + billing + finance in one platform

Spaire is not only a tax/compliance layer.

It includes:

- Product catalog and pricing configuration
- Subscriptions, usage billing, and lifecycle automation
- Checkout and customer portal flows
- Entitlements and post-purchase access automation
- Balance visibility and payout operations

This unifies legal, financial, and product monetization workflows in one system.

## Who Spaire is built for

Spaire is designed for SaaS startups and digital software businesses that want to:

- Sell globally from day one
- Reduce tax and compliance overhead
- Avoid stitching together multiple billing vendors
- Keep lean teams operating at enterprise-grade standards
