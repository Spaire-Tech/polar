As your Merchant of Record, Spaire is the legal seller on every transaction processed through the platform. This means we share responsibility for what gets sold. We focus exclusively on **digital products and software** — we do not support physical goods or purely human-delivered services.

## What you can sell

- **Software & SaaS** — web apps, desktop apps, mobile apps, CLI tools, APIs
- **Digital products** — templates, fonts, icons, design assets, code libraries, eBooks, PDFs, video courses, audio content, stock media
- **Licenses & access** — software licenses, gated content, community access, digital subscriptions

**The general rule:** your product must be digital, deliverable immediately (or via your own system), and something you'd confidently describe to a bank or regulator without hesitation.

## What you cannot sell

<Note>
This list is not exhaustive. Spaire reserves the right to review, restrict, or suspend accounts at any time if we determine the usage is deceptive, high-risk, or harmful to consumers.
</Note>

**Illegal or regulated**
- Anything illegal in the jurisdictions you operate in or sell to — for example, selling unlicensed financial tools into France or Germany where financial advice regulations apply
- Products that violate payment network rules (Visa, Mastercard, Stripe's restricted businesses list)
- Regulated financial services — trading bots, investment signals, brokerage tools, crypto assets

**Harmful or deceptive**
- Fake testimonials, review inflation platforms, or social proof manipulation
- "Get rich quick" content or schemes
- Products using misleading claims about results or outcomes
- Malware, spyware, or tools designed to compromise systems

**Adult & age-restricted**
- Adult content of any kind, including AI-generated
- Products targeted at or accessible to minors
- Age-restricted goods (alcohol, tobacco, vaping products)

**Physical & human services**
- Physical goods of any kind
- Consulting, design, marketing, or development services sold as a product
- Anything requiring human fulfilment that isn't digital and immediate

**Marketplace & resale**
- Selling other people's products or services through Spaire
- Reselling software licenses you don't own or aren't authorized to redistribute
- Products where you don't hold the required IP rights or distribution licenses

**Other prohibited categories**
- Donations or charity-style transfers (no product exchange)
- Gambling, lotteries, mystery boxes, or randomized reward mechanics
- IPTV services
- NFTs and crypto assets
- Telecommunication or eSIM services
- Third-party content downloaders (YouTube rippers, Instagram scrapers, etc.)
- API or IP cloaking services
- Medical advice, pharmaceutical, or weight loss products
- Pseudo-science services (horoscopes, fortune-telling, clairvoyance)
- Travel reservations, timeshares, or travel clubs
- Watermark removal tools
- Advertising automation, lead scraping, or bulk outreach tools
- Services that circumvent rules or rate limits of other platforms

## Restricted categories

These are allowed but require closer review before activation. We apply a higher bar for quality, trust, and compliance:

- **Directories & boards** — must have a clear legitimate purpose; not primarily selling ad placement
- **Marketing services** — must not offer spam, mass outreach, or unsolicited contact features
- **Pre-orders & paid waitlists** — high-risk by nature; we may withhold payouts until fulfilment is verified
- **Ticket sales** — event and access tickets on a case-by-case basis
- **eBooks** — accepted, but AI-generated or low-effort content is not

## Common questions

**Can I sell a SaaS product to customers in France?**

Yes. Spaire handles VAT collection and remittance for EU transactions including France. You focus on your product; Spaire manages the tax compliance.

**Can I offer a paid waitlist to validate my idea before building?**

Generally no. Pre-orders are high-risk because consumers can dispute the charge if the product isn't delivered as described. For established teams with a track record, exceptions may be considered — [contact us](/support) to discuss your case.

**Can I sell a course with consulting calls included?**

The digital course component is fine. If the consulting or coaching element is the primary value being sold, that moves into human services territory which we don't support. A course with optional office hours as a minor add-on is typically acceptable.

**Not sure if your product qualifies?**

[Reach out before you launch](/support). It's faster to get a quick answer than to build something out and find out it's not supported.
